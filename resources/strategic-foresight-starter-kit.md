# Strategic Foresight Starter Kit

Use this worksheet to begin a practical foresight conversation with your team.

## 1. Define the focal question

What decision, strategy, or operating challenge needs clearer future context?

## 2. Capture signals of change

- What new behaviors are emerging among clients, communities, or partners?
- What technologies, regulations, funding shifts, or social expectations are changing?
- Which signals feel weak now but could become important later?

## 3. Name assumptions

- What are we assuming will stay stable?
- What are we assuming about demand, capacity, leadership, or trust?
- Which assumptions would be costly if wrong?

## 4. Build strategic options

List three moves the organization could make if the environment changes faster, slower, or differently than expected.

## 5. Choose next learning steps

Identify the evidence, conversations, or experiments that would reduce uncertainty before committing.
