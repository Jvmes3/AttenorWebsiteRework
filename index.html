AI automation proposal

Automation support for content planning and email follow-through.

Two intelligent agent workflows help keep strategic content moving and daily inbox tasks visible across Gmail and Outlook.

01 Content Agent

Calendar-gated content generation

The agent checks Google Calendar each day, activates only on scheduled content days, reads the event brief, researches current sources, and saves a formatted caption, post body, and source list to Google Drive.

  • Uses talking points, subject focus, audience, tone, and notes
  • Supports futures thinking, strategic foresight, and scenario building
  • Includes source links for attribution, credits, and photo research
02 Email Agent

Morning task summary across inboxes

A scheduled morning workflow reviews new Gmail and Outlook messages, labels them by priority or action, extracts tasks, and creates one unified summary with sender, inbox source, deadline, and next step.

  • Identifies replies, approvals, follow-ups, and deadlines
  • Specifies who sent each task and whether it came from Gmail or Outlook
  • Can save summaries to Drive or deliver them by email
