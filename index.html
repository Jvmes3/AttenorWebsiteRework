Foresight grounded in evidence. Coaching grounded in action.

01

Strategic Foresight

Most leaders are making today's decisions with yesterday's thinking. Strategic foresight helps you lift your head from the immediate and read what is emerging in your industry before your competitors do. We scan signals, build scenarios, and help you position for the future you want to lead into, not just the one that is arriving uninvited.

02

Research and Evaluation

Good decisions require honest information. We design and facilitate the research and evaluation processes that surface what is actually happening inside your organization and among the people you serve. The findings do not just sit in a report. They become the foundation for your next move.

03

Executive Coaching

This is not accountability coaching or goal tracking. This is the conversation that helps a leader see themselves clearly, name what has been in the way, and move with intention toward the future they are building. For leaders who are ready to be challenged, not just supported.

What sets the work apart

Collaborative strategy built from real evidence.

Attenor Collaborative works closely with clients to co-create useful, credible strategy. Engagements are designed to move from discovery to insight to execution, with enough structure to make progress and enough flexibility to adapt as new information appears.

  • Research plans aligned to practical decisions
  • Facilitated sensemaking for leadership teams
  • Clear recommendations, not just raw findings
  • Ongoing support as teams implement and learn
